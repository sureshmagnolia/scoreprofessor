🔒 Serverless & Private

This is a completely serverless application. All your entered data is saved securely and exclusively on your computer's local browser storage. No data is ever sent to or stored on any external server. To ensure you don't lose your data if you clear your browser history or switch computers, we strongly urge you to take regular backups using the ⬇ Export Backup (JSON) feature. You can easily restore your data later using the ⬆ Load Backup (JSON) button.
